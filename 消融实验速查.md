## 论文中如何写消融

### MOSI

> Ablation analysis (Table X) reveals that text-only achieves the highest ACC2 (88.11%), marginally exceeding full TAV (87.80%, Δ=+0.31pp within seed variation). Removing audio or vision corrections each reduces ACC2 by 0.25pp. The reliability gate and Hadamard interaction show negligible effects (-0.05pp and -0.10pp, respectively), as the correction magnitudes themselves are small (|ŷ - y_text| ≈ 0.01).

### MOSEI

> Under a fixed 4-epoch protocol, three ablation effects reach statistical significance (p<0.05, paired bootstrap 10K): (1) removing Hadamard interactions reduces ACC2 by 2.37pp (95% CI [+1.49, +3.25]), identifying them as the most critical AWAF component; (2) removing vision reduces ACC2 by 1.67pp (95% CI [+0.61, +2.73]); (3) global static weights outperform sample-level dynamic AWAF by 0.97pp (95% CI [+0.15, +1.79]).
