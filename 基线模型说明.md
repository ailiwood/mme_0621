## 各模型简介

### TFN-lite (Tensor Fusion Network)
- **原文**: Zadeh et al., "Tensor Fusion Network for Multimodal Sentiment Analysis", EMNLP 2017
- **代码**: `models/baselines/tfn_lite.py`
- **原理**: 对三模态向量计算外积，生成多模态张量表示，再通过全连接层融合
- **lite版差异**: 简化了张量分解维度，使用 128-dim hidden（原文更大）

### LMF-lite (Low-rank Multimodal Fusion)
- **原文**: Liu et al., "Efficient Low-rank Multimodal Fusion with Modality-Specific Factors", ACL 2018
- **代码**: `models/baselines/lmf_lite.py`
- **原理**: 使用低秩分解近似 TFN 的外积张量，减少参数量
- **lite版差异**: rank=16（原文可能更大），hidden_dim=128

### MulT-lite (Multimodal Transformer)
- **原文**: Tsai et al., "Multimodal Transformer for Unaligned Multimodal Language Sequences", ACL 2019
- **代码**: `models/baselines/mult_lite.py`
- **原理**: 使用跨模态 Transformer 注意力进行模态间对齐和融合
- **lite版差异**: 1层 cross-attention（原文多层），hidden_dim=128

### SelfMM-lite (Self-Supervised Multi-task Multimodal)
- **原文**: Yu et al., "Learning Modality-Specific Representations with Self-Supervised Multi-Task Learning", AAAI 2021
- **代码**: `models/baselines/self_mm_lite.py`
- **原理**: 模态特定编码 + 自监督对比学习 + 多任务训练
- **lite版差异**: 简化了自监督目标，hidden_dim=128

### MISA-lite (Modality-Invariant and Modality-Specific Representations)
- **原文**: Hazarika et al., "MISA: Modality-Invariant and -Specific Representations for Multimodal Sentiment Analysis", ACM MM 2020
- **代码**: `models/baselines/misa_lite.py`
- **原理**: 模态不变编码器 + 模态特定编码器 + 分布对齐损失
- **lite版差异**: 简化了对抗训练和分布对齐模块

### MLCL-lite & DLF-lite
- 简化实现的多模态对比学习和动态融合模型
- 代码: `models/baselines/mlcl_lite.py`, `models/baselines/dlf_lite.py`
